Strip Minstry Of Waxing

Project Details

The global leaders in waxing & laser for both men & women. The brief was to design the campaign for its India launch in Mumbai. Hence, we at Spak took cues from their existing creative palette and came up with something quirky and cool that would continue with the brand visual identity. The client in Mumbai, loved what we created and delivered. We designed, launch ads, hoardings, in-store, banners, key-tags, window, digital.
