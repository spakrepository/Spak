Hamleys Come & Play Campaign

Project Details

The “Come & Play” with Hamleys was designed to entice new kids to walk-into any Hamleys and experience the new products. The engagement with the kids and the products at Hamleys would definitely convert these new customers into loyal ones! The visuals showcased are of the brilliant products from Hamleys with young and energetic kids! Various collaterals were designed for this campaign, right from hoardings, standees, posters, banners, social, etc.
