Solehead Campaign Design

Project Details

Solehead is a new urban footwear brand for women, with its products being more chic, urban and Indian. The brief was to conceptualise the entire launch campaign as more chic, elegant & sexy! The launch face for this campaign was Rachel White and she was perfect for this campaign! Various collaterals were designed for this like Hoardings, Banners, Standees, POS, Print Ad.
