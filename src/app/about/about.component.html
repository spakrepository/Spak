About Us

We are a branding, design & digital creative agency. We bring newbrands to life & breathe new life into existing ones.

Our artistry encompasses both print & digital applications across a variety of industry sectors, from corporate businesses all the way through to consumer facing brands.

From our design studio in the city of Mumbai, we consistently deliver the exceptionally high quality of work for which we are becoming renowned.

Our team’s individual talents align to offer a comprehensive array of commercially focussed creativity.

‘Spak Studios’ had opened in 2008 from a tiny apartment with 2 people only as a design studio! It was 2012, that Spak Studios transformed into a full-fledged branding & design agency and was redesigned as Spak Communications after 4-5 years of design & marketing experience working alongside some of the India’s most successful & prestigious brands.

Our blend of strategy & creativity results in dynamic outcomes that assist in supporting the future growth of our clients’ businesses. Everything we achieve is repeatedly delivered efficiently through commitment, sincerity & unconditional professionalism.

The Spak culture brings together far more than just great talent.

Agency culture is of paramount importance. Therefore at Spakcomm you will benefit from our team of creatively accomplished, logically gifted, genuine folk, who are all obsessive in their quest for design perfection.

We have no time for pretentious, overbearing, prima donnas. We respect our clients, each other, as well as ourselves.

We thrive in our energetic, collaborative environment – we believe this yields the high quality & consistency of work for which we are becoming renowned.

Quite simply; outstanding work, crafted by a team of genuine folk.
